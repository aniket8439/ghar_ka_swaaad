Craze in India

Indian food, with its hodgepodge of ingredients and intoxicating aromas, is coveted around
the world. The labor-intensive cuisine and its mix of spices is more often than not a
revelation for those who sit down to eat it for the first time. Heavy doses of cardamom,
cayenne, tamarind and other flavors can overwhelm an unfamiliar palate. Together, they
help form the pillars of what tastes so good to so many people.
